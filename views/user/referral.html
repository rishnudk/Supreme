How Our Referral Program Works

Earn rewards by inviting your friends, family, and colleagues to join our platform. It's simple to get started:

1

Share Your Link

Copy your unique referral link or code and share it with friends through email, social media, or messaging apps.

2

Friends Sign Up

When your friends click your link or use your code during registration, they're connected to your account.

3

Earn Rewards

For each friend who successfully signs up and completes their first transaction, you both receive rewards.

Benefits

  • Earn $25 for each successful referral who completes their first transaction
  • Your referred friends get a $15 bonus when they sign up
  • Unlock exclusive rewards when you reach referral milestones
  • No limit on how many people you can refer
